BUT, WHO ARE WE?

We are an empowered, co-located team of designers, developers, analysts, and managers located in the London Studio. We use the latest technologies and practices to design and build things that make people’s lives easier. We operate in a transparent and flat organization where everyone’s ideas are valued and we’re able to learn from each other in a fun and experimental studio atmosphere. As an award-winning team, we have been recognised internally for our success but we remain dedicated to continuously improving and growing alongside the London technology ecosystem.
